Howdy, I am Manuel!

My passion for understanding the complexities of our world began during my undergraduate studies in economics. As I delved deeper into the subject, I became fascinated with the question of why some countries and companies were able to thrive while others struggled. I was particularly intrigued by the role that technology played in shaping these outcomes. Through my studies, I developed a strong analytical mindset that I have since applied to every area of my life. I view the world through the lens of scarcity, seeking to understand how resources are managed and how policies are developed.
My analytical curiosity was truly awakened during this time, and I developed a deep passion for asking the right questions and gathering the most accurate data possible. To me, data is not just a series of numbers or metrics - it's a story waiting to be told. I believe that by analyzing and interpreting data, we can uncover valuable insights that can be used to develop innovative products and services that truly make a difference.
I am excited to continue my journey as a data analyst and look forward to sharing my insights and experiences with you on this website. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit more about me!
I truly believe that the most enticing projects happen during collaboration. If you want to work together please don't hesitate to contact me!
